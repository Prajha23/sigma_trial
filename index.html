How We Do It

A specialized team "Social Research Centre (SRC) team"
meticulously plans the design of the research study
in accordance to client requirements. Did u know this team has helped
plan out over 600 studies involving the entire Indian population.

Then comes the "Field and Tab Unit (FTU) team", they execute the research/study on field with all the relevant
tools they would require to collect data accurately and efficiently.
These guys have completed over 1000 studies till date in every possible location across the Motherland-India

Finally comes all the organizing of the chunk of data
collected and making sense of it, hence the team "Data Processing Unit".
They analyze the data with different statistical methodologies and data analysis tools.
